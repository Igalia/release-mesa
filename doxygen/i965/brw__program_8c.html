Sets up the starting offsets for the groups of binding table entries common to all pipeline stages.

Unused groups are initialized to 0xd0d0d0d0 to make it obvious that they're unused but also make sure that addition of small offsets to them will trigger some of our asserts that surface indices are < BRW_MAX_SURFACES.
