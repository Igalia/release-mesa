§ ShareGroupReset

bool gl_shared_state::ShareGroupReset

Some context in this share group was affected by a GPU reset.

On the next call to glGetGraphicsResetStatus, contexts that have not been affected by a GPU reset must also return GL_INNOCENT_CONTEXT_RESET_ARB.

Once this field becomes true, it is never reset to false.
