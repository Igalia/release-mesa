Detailed Description

IR lower pass to perform the division of texture coordinates by the texture projector if present.

Many GPUs have a texture sampling opcode that takes the projector and does the divide internally, thus the presence of the projector in the IR. For GPUs that don't, this saves the driver needing the logic for handling the divide.
