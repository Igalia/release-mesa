NIR Control Flow Modification.

This file contains various APIs that make modifying control flow in NIR, while maintaining the invariants checked by the validator, much easier. There are two parts to this:

  1. Inserting control flow (ifs and loops) in various places, for creating IR either from scratch or as part of some lowering pass.
  2. Taking existing pieces of the IR and either moving them around or deleting them.Control flow insertion. puts a control flow node where the cursor is
