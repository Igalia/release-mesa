When the GS is not in use, we assign the entire URB space to the VS.

When the GS is in use, we split the URB space evenly between the VS and the GS. This is not ideal, but it's simple. 

      URB size / 2                   URB size / 2

_____________-______________ _____________-______________ / \ / \ +----------------------------------------------------------—+ | Vertex Shader Entries | Geometry Shader Entries | +----------------------------------------------------------—+

Sandybridge GT1 has 32kB of URB space, while GT2 has 64kB. (See the Sandybridge PRM, Volume 2, Part 1, Section 1.4.7: 3DSTATE_URB.)
